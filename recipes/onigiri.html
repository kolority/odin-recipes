Onigiri

Onigiri is a japanese food made from white rice formed into triangular or cylindrical shapes and often wrapped in nori.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Nori
  • Choice of filling

Steps

  1. Use freshly cooked rice
  2. Wet your hands
  3. Salt your hands
  4. Use just enough pressure to form desired shape
  5. Wrap in nori
