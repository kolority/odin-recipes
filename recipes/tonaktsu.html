Tonkatsu

Tonkatsu is a dish consisting of breaded, deep-fried pork cutlets.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Salt and pepper
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Neutral oil
  • Tonkatsu Sauce

Steps

  1. Moisten panko
  2. Make slits on connective tissues of the pork
  3. Pound the pork
  4. Double fry
  5. Clean oil between batches and repeat
